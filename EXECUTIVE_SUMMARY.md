## How the Contact Form Will Work

1. **Visitor fills out contact form** on your website
2. **Form is submitted** with spam protection
3. **You receive an email** instantly with their information:
   - Name, email, phone
   - Property address (if provided)
   - Their message
4. **Visitor receives confirmation** email that their message was sent
5. **You respond directly** to their email address

### Example Email You'll Receive:
```
Subject: New Contact Form Submission from KTH Website

Name: John Smith
Email: john@example.com
Phone: (555) 123-4567
Property: 123 Main St, City, State

Message:
I'm interested in learning more about your property
management services for my rental home...

---
Sent from KTH Property Management Website
Date: November 5, 2025 at 2:30 PM
```
